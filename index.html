About us

BYT is a startup company where you can book training camps and sports facilities for different types of sport events. Event organizers can make a user and use our event management systems to create events, search and book our partner, and manage the whole process - everything that an event manager needs.


Intention of this part is to make Camp booking easier and quicker. BYT is reserved for trainers/coaches only. It’s a tool that makes their camp bookings and event organizing easier.


It’s a complex system that will allow a trainer to book their camp within minutes. Platform will provide the possibility to set up a camp from camp site selection up to accommodation and catering services.
